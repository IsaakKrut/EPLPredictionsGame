This game is based on predictions made before the beginning of 
English Premier League Football season.

1. Each player picks 6 teams in order and places them on position from 1 to 6, 
based on the position the player expects them to be at after the last match of the season.
2. For each team, a player receives points, based on the following formula :
5 -|Xe - Xr|, where Xe is the position the player have chosen for that team and Xr is the position they actually finished in.
3. The player with the most points wins.
